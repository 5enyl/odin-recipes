Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is simple to make and freezer friendly which makes it perfect for meal prep or to plan ahead! It includes ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and a meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Instructions

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a casserole dish and add 1/3rd of the boiled lasagna noodles
  2. Spread 1/3rd of the ricotta cheese mixture on top and add 1.5 cups of meat sauce
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 two more times
  4. Cover the top with mozzarella and bake at 375 for 30 minutes covered
  5. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes, broiling after if the cheese is not melted
